kiiiisu

 

Signe is the team’s sunshine with lots of positive and motivational attitude, even if the game is going bad. She understands very well the importance of cooperation, and her jokes and catchphrases always bring energetic, good atmosphere into the match. After 5 years of playing CS 1.6, she decided to try the League of Legends and immediately got hooked. Her favourite champions are Cho’gath and Jarvan IV. Kiiiisu and her team hope to prove that it is possible for female teams to fight with male rivals head to head.
